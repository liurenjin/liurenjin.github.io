Maven自动部署war包至Tomcat

Maven3.2.5 Tomcat6

插件说明与配置

具体细节可以查看参考资料进行推敲。
1. tomcat-maven-plugin插件已经从groupId的org.codehaus.mojo转至org.apache.tomcat.maven;
2. pom.xml配置： 

    <plugin>
       <groupId>org.apache.tomcat.maven</groupId>
       <artifactId>tomcat6-maven-plugin</artifactId>
       <version>2.2</version>
       <configuration>
       <url>http://localhost:8080/manager</url>
       <uriEncoding>UTF-8</uriEncoding>
       <server>demoServer</server>
       <!-- <username>demo</username> <password>demo</password> -->
       <update>true</update>
       <!-- application path always starts with / -->
       <path>/${finalName}</path>
       </configuration>         
    </plugin>

其中project的节点下配置<finalName>${project.artifactId}</finalName>
